What is ESG Center Built for

The main pain point problems addressed by this product are the relatively complicated information disclosed in the reports, which creates obstacles to reading and filtering information; the lack of a uniform and standardized format, which is not conducive to horizontal comparison; and the limited number and efficiency during manual processing of ESG reports.

To address such problems, the business goal of this product is to automatically process ESG reports in large quantities through computer technology, realize the extraction of key words and key graphs from ESG reports based on key indicators such as word frequency, and automatically generate summaries to help users establish a preliminary understanding of the ESG work of multiple companies more conveniently and efficiently. And based on big data analysis, it draws more in-depth conclusions and realizes more diversified functions.
